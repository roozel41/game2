Gameplay
----------
Destrying floating items with your finger.

Worlds
-------
- water world  - a shot of a water with some bottom background ( a destroyed ship etc. )
- air world    - clouds, earth deep below ( some fields, a small house, trees )

Effects
--------
Depending on the world, when pressing a finger to the screen, a different entity
follows the finger, leaving a trail of particles:
- water world -   shark - a cool wave trail
- air world   -   airplane - gust of air / smoke

Destructibles
-------------
- water world -    meduzas, floating treasures, fishes ( the latter can run from the shark )
- air world   -    baloons, birds etc.
